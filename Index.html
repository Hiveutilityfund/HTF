Executive Summary

Hive Utility Fund (HTF) is an ETF-styled cryptocurrency fund built on the Solana blockchain. Unlike speculative tokens that rely on hype, HTF strategically invests in proven utility-driven Solana tokens with real-world applications. As these investments appreciate in value, the fund liquidates profits and reinvests them into buybacks, burns, token holder dividends, and ecosystem growth.
